### **What is KiwiHacks?** {#what-is-kiwihacks?}

KiwiHacks is a [*hackathon*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hackathon) (coding event) *organised by high schoolers in Auckland,* with chaperones\! 

KiwiHacks is happening at the GridAKL John Lysaght Startup Space, May 2nd-3rd, from 11am on Saturday to 11am on Sunday.  
Your child will be making either a software or hardware project over 24 hours. There’ll be plenty of resources to help them, such as 3D printers, hardware, and the many free workshops we will run during the event.

### **Expenses & Meals** {#expenses-meals}

Attending KiwiHacks will be completely **free** for your child\!   
This includes meals, snacks and drinks.  
We will also provide free swag to all attendees.  
That being said, there are some things which your child needs to bring with them.   
A full packing list can be found [here](?tab=t.km9mby6vm6bd).

### **Sleeping Arrangements**

There will be designated gender-separated sleeping areas inside the venue, likely camping-style on the floor. As such, attendees may bring a foam mattress topper if they prefer for increased comfort.  

### **Facilities**

Aside from open plan workspaces and the main space, the venue has large, clean bathrooms with toilets and sinks, as well as a kitchenette.   
The main event space will be cleared for sleeping, as detailed above.  
There are no showers, so deodorant is strongly recommended.

### **Safety Arrangements** {#safety-arrangements}

We are committed to creating a safe and comfortable environment for students at KiwiHacks.  
The event will have 24/7 adult supervision. The venue is closed doors, meaning only registered guests will be allowed in, as it is keycard entry. Additionally, we have a zero-tolerance policy towards any drugs, alcohol, or any other forms of contraband.
